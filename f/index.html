Selected Work

Research & Projects

Quantitative research and financial modelling projects focused on systematic investment strategies and macroeconomic forecasting.

01 — Systematic Equity Strategy

Long-Short Factor Investing Strategy

A market-neutral long-short equity strategy harvesting Value, Momentum, and Quality risk premia on the S&P 500 universe. Built with MSCI-aligned composite scoring, sector-neutral z-score normalisation, and Betting-Against-Beta (BAB) construction to isolate factor exposure from market beta. Backtested from 2005 to 2026 with monthly rebalancing.

Python EODHD API HDF5 Backtesting

02 — Macroeconomic Forecasting

Inflation Forecasting via Regularised ML

Evaluated whether regularised machine learning models — Ridge, Lasso, and Elastic Net — can systematically outperform Central Bank analyst consensus on CPI forecasting. Models trained on 100+ macroeconomic indicators with walk-forward validation to prevent look-ahead bias, benchmarked against the official market consensus.

Python Central Bank API Scikit-Learn
↗
03 — Risk Analytics

Equity Risk Analytics Dashboard (CAPM)

Browser-based interactive tool for single-stock and portfolio risk analysis under the CAPM framework. Computes rolling beta, expected return, and visualises the Security Market Line for any S&P 500 stock — powered by live Yahoo Finance and EODHD data.

Python Yahoo Finance EODHD API
